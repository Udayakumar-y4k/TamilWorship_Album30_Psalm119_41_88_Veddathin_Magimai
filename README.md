Psalm 119 (Vav to Lamed sections)

A deep prayer series for God’s Word, deliverance, hope, and steadfast love.

Study Note:
Psalm 119:41-96 continues the longest chapter in the Bible. In these sections the psalmist prays for God’s salvation, mercy, hope, and strength to obey. 
Key themes: Trusting in God’s promises, walking in liberty, and finding comfort in His law during affliction.

Prayer Focus:
Lord, let Your steadfast love and salvation come to me according to Your Word. Help me to walk in Your truth with freedom and hope. Amen.
